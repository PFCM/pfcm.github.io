Paul Mathews

Projects—

Tangle
Network for performance and composition with sophisticated instruments. Written in ChucK, work undertaken for a Summer Research Scholarship at Victoria University, School of Engineering and Computer Science, supervised by Ajay Kapur and Dale Carnegie.
Immanence – collaboration with Timothy Barraclough
An audiovisual/electromechanical installation, with Timothy Barraclough.
Live at X

A series of live electronic performances exploring the performer-audience relationships which constitute a musical performance, with a view towards testing the limits engagement by providing a sonic exploration of the audible architecture of the space in which is is performed. This piece has been performed in several incarnations.

Live at St Paul's Cathedral
Performance as part of a larger concert in the Wellington Cathedral of St Paul.
